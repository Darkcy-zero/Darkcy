Frequently Asked Questions

1.What is C’s Café & Pastry’s?

C’s Café & Pastry’s is a cozy café that offers freshly brewed coffee and delicious baked bread made with care and quality ingredients.

2. What kind of drinks do you serve?

We serve a variety of coffee drinks such as Americano, Latte, Mocha, Espresso, and Frappe.

3. Do you also offer baked goods?

Yes. Along with our coffee, we offer a selection of freshly baked bread to perfectly pair with your drinks.

4. Are your products freshly prepared?

Yes. Our drinks are freshly brewed, and our bread is prepared to ensure quality, taste, and freshness.
