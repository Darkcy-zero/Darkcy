About Us


At C’s Café & Pastry’s, we offer freshly made pastries and a cozy café experience. Here’s what you can expect from us:



  • Baked Pastries- Soft breads, cookies, and homemade treats baked daily.

  • Specialty Coffee & Drinks- Hot and iced beverages crafted for your comfort.

  • Custom Orders– Cakes, pastry boxes, and personalized requests for any occasion.

  • Dine-In & Takeout– Whether you stay or go, we serve your order warm and fresh.

Our goal is to bring you simple comfort food that feels close to home.
